This is a chart to help with the status bar. The status bar is
5 rows with each row spanning 30 8x8 tiles. The status bar tile
data lines up with this chart. The position is stored during
DMA to ADDRESS_FOR_VRAM_READ_WRITE, and the data size is byte 6
of DMA_1_PARAMS. Bytes 3-5 of DMA_1_PARAMS are a 24-bit pointer
to the tile data. Tiles past $9F are reserved for message boxes
and other layer 3 data.
